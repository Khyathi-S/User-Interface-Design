Top Tourist Destinations in India
Goa: Known for its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, and rich Portuguese heritage.

Agra: Home to the iconic Taj Mahal, one of the Seven Wonders of the World.

Delhi: The capital city, rich in history and culture, with landmarks like the Red Fort and India Gate.

Jaipur: The "Pink City" of Rajasthan, famous for its palaces and forts.

Mumbai: The bustling financial capital, known for Bollywood, beaches, and historical sites.

Chennai: A cultural hub with temples, museums, and a vibrant arts scene.

Bengaluru: The tech city, offering a mix of modernity and green spaces.

Hyderabad: Known for its rich history, cuisine, and the iconic Charminar.

Kochi: A port city in Kerala, famous for its backwaters and colonial architecture.

Udaipur: The "City of Lakes" in Rajasthan, known for its scenic lakes and palaces.

Shimla: A charming hill station in Himachal Pradesh, perfect for a summer retreat.

Manali: Another hill station, popular for adventure sports and scenic beauty.

Darjeeling: Famous for its tea gardens and views of the Himalayas.

Ooty: The "Queen of Hill Stations" in Tamil Nadu, known for its lush landscapes.

Varanasi: A spiritual city on the banks of the Ganges, known for its ghats and temples.

Kolkata: The cultural capital, known for its literary heritage and colonial architecture.

Amritsar: Home to the Golden Temple, a significant Sikh pilgrimage site.

Puducherry: A former French colony with beautiful beaches and ashrams.

Jaisalmer: Known for its golden fort and desert landscapes.
